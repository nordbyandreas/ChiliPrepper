About ChiliPrepper

ChiliPrepper is a mobile web app and teaching assistant.

ChiliPrepper was created as part of a software development course at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU).

The team consists of 4 students.

Check out the link below for a short video guide on how to use ChiliPrepper (make sure you're connected to the internet)
